# CLAUDE.md - Project Instructions

## Course Overview
AI for Brainstorming & Ideation - A writingIO mini course on creative thinking and problem-solving with AI.

## Audience
- Professionals facing challenges
- Teams working on projects
- Anyone wanting to think more creatively
- People stuck in familiar patterns

## Tone and Style
- Energetic and encouraging
- Emphasizes possibility and exploration
- Practical with actionable techniques

## Video Script Guidelines
- Brand name is "writingIO" (not "Writing IO" or "writing.io")
- Spell out numbers and acronyms for speech (e.g., "A I" not "AI")
- Scripts must be under 5000 characters for HeyGen
- First lesson includes Amanda intro and AI disclosure

## Key Themes
- Quantity before quality in ideation
- Multiple perspectives reveal new options
- Explore deeply before deciding
- Ideas are only valuable if acted upon
