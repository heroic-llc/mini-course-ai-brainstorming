# AI for Brainstorming & Ideation

A writingIO mini course on using AI for creative thinking and problem-solving.

## Course Overview

Learn how to use AI to generate more ideas, explore options thoroughly, and solve problems creatively.

## Lessons

1. **AI for Creative Thinking** - Introduction to AI-powered brainstorming
2. **Generating Ideas** - Techniques for producing lots of options
3. **Exploring Options** - Going deeper on promising ideas
4. **Creative Problem-Solving** - Finding solutions you wouldn't think of alone
5. **From Ideas to Action** - Selecting and implementing your best ideas

## Target Audience

- Professionals facing challenges that need creative solutions
- Teams working on projects and initiatives
- Anyone who wants to think more creatively
- People who get stuck in familiar thinking patterns
