# AI for Brainstorming & Ideation

## Lesson 2: Generating Ideas

### Overview
Quantity before quality. AI helps you generate lots of ideas quickly.

---

### Techniques for Generating Ideas

**Ask for volume**
- Request 10 or 20 ideas, not 3
- AI doesn't get tired

**Request variety**
- Practical ideas, creative ideas, wild ideas
- Different categories expand range

**Change perspectives**
- "How would a child approach this?"
- "What would a scientist suggest?"

**Build on ideas**
- Generate variations of interesting ideas

**Remove constraints**
- Unlimited budget, time, no rules
- Adapt wild ideas to reality

**Go opposite**
- Reverse your goal
- Sometimes sparks new thinking
