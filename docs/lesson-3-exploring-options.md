# AI for Brainstorming & Ideation

## Lesson 3: Exploring Options

### Overview
After generating ideas, explore the most promising ones more deeply.

---

### How to Explore Options

**Expand on specifics**
- What would this look like in practice?
- What are the first steps?

**Examine from multiple angles**
- What's great about this?
- What could go wrong?
- What's missing?

**Consider stakeholders**
- Who would be affected?
- What would different groups think?

**Explore variations**
- Smaller, bigger, faster, simpler versions

**Test with scenarios**
- How does it work in different situations?

**Identify requirements**
- What's needed to make it work?

**Compare options**
- What are the tradeoffs?
