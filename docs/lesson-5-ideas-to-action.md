# AI for Brainstorming & Ideation

## Lesson 5: From Ideas to Action

### Overview
Brainstorming is valuable only if it leads somewhere. AI helps you select and implement ideas.

---

### Moving to Action

**Evaluate against criteria**
- What matters most?
- Rate ideas against your criteria

**Identify quick wins**
- What can you test or implement fastest?

**Create action steps**
- Break ideas into concrete next steps

**Anticipate obstacles**
- What could go wrong?
- How can you prepare?

**Build a case**
- Present ideas persuasively to others

**Plan for iteration**
- Design ways to test and improve

**Document your thinking**
- Summarize the process and decisions
