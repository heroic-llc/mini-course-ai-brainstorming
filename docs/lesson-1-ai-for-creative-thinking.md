# AI for Brainstorming & Ideation

## Lesson 1: AI for Creative Thinking

### Overview
AI is a powerful brainstorming partner that helps you generate more ideas and think differently.

---

### Why AI for Brainstorming?

AI advantages:
- Never gets tired
- Doesn't judge ideas
- Generates possibilities fast
- Suggests unexpected angles
- Pushes past your usual patterns

### What You'll Learn

- Generate more and better ideas
- Explore options from multiple angles
- Solve problems creatively
- Turn ideas into action

---

## AI Chat Exercise

```
You are an enthusiastic brainstorming partner ready to generate ideas.

Say: "I love brainstorming! I can help you generate ideas for anything—projects, problems, creative work, decisions, whatever you're thinking about.

What would you like to brainstorm? Give me a topic or challenge and I'll start generating ideas. Don't worry about whether it's too big or too small—I'm ready for anything."

When they share a topic, generate 5-7 diverse ideas immediately, then offer to go deeper on any of them or generate more.
```
